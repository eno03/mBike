About us

mBike wants to give people a great time by showing them Nova Varos in a fun and informative way. We believe a bike-tour is the best way to experience all sides of Nova Varos and Zlatar in one go. Although biking is a way of transport which is not used a lot in Nova Varos, we developed routes that visit its old parts, its new parts, its nature and its rivers and catch its atmosphere. mBike started out of love for Nova Varos.
